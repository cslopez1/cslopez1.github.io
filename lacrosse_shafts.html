Lacrosse Shaft Design

Worked and collaborated with the design team at Alpha Lacrosse to design these lacrosse shafts for the University of California Men's Lacrosse team. Developed several mockups inspired by my on-field experiences, then commissioned the design team to assemble several prototypes. I then surveyed my team officers and captains to determine which design was the most popular. Once a clear winner was established, we initiated production, and they are currently being used by our entire team.
