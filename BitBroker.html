BitBroker UX Design Internship

I worked at this San Francisco-based start-up while finishing my undergraduate degree at UC Berkeley in Spring of 2019. Funded by Berkeley's Skydeck Accelerator (past home of the $1+ billion unicorn LimeBike, and Kiwi Robotics), Bitbroker applies machine learning to improve the cryptocurrency community, and creates new usages of crypto in the coming blockchain economy.

My roles as a UX Designer include designing the company logos, as well as creating user flows, wireframes, and prototypes critical to the development of the startup's primary website and other products.
